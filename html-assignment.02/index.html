About Me

My name is Daria, and I am 18 years old. I was born on December 11th in a small town of Russia. Now I've been living for 1 month in Ottawa. I am a first-year student at Algonquin College studying interactive media design. I am passionate about creating new arts and designs, especially when it comes to websites and posters.

In my free time, I enjoy listening to music as music allows me to immerse myself in different melodies and rhythms. I am also interested in taking photos of nature, buildings, landscapes, food, and people. Photography helps me capture special moments and express my creativity through visual storytelling. In addition, exploring new places, whether it's my hometown or completely new place, brings me joy and fulfillment to life.
